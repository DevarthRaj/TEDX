About The Event

What is TEDx?

TEDx is a grassroots initiative, created in the spirit of TED's overall mission to research and discover "ideas worth spreading." TEDx brings the spirit of TED to local communities around the globe through independently organized events.

Our event features live speakers and TED Talk videos that spark deep conversation and connections. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.
